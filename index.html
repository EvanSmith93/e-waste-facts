What is E-Waste?

The term e-waste refers to electronics that are no longer wanted or usable. This includes old computers, TVs, phones, and other devices.

Why is e-waste so bad?

E-waste is often thrown away with the regular trash, which can leach chemicals into the soil and water. When electronics are recycled, they're often sent to under-developed countries. Locals pull apart and burn the electronics to extract minerals, which releases toxic chemicals into the air.
