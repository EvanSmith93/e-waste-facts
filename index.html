About E-Waste

E-waste refers to any electronic product that is no longer being used. This includes computers, TVs, phones, and other electronic devices. These products are often thrown away with the regular trash, which can leach chemicals into the soil and water. When electronics are recycled, they're often sent to under-developed countries. Locals pull apart and burn the electronics to extract minerals, which releases toxic chemicals into the air.
